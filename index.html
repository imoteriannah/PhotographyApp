Our Brief History

Imotech Photography Nigeria Limited started operations as a small studio shop offering to take and edit pictures and moments people wanted to keep for memory purposes. Down the line, the company evolved out of the need to meet demand and to extend its reach in the market and with this expansion came a need for better equipment, a more focused goal and to create an online presence for our community of clients. In 2020, the company acquired a bigger space where we are currently and pushed operations internationally to cover any event anywhere on the planet
